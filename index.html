Record Outlaw

"Record Outlaw" is an exhilarating adventure that follows a renegade DJ on a quest to break all musical boundaries and defy the oppressive forces of the industry. Fueled by rebellion and an unwavering passion for music, this high-stakes tale explores the power of art to challenge the status quo and redefine what it means to be a true musical pioneer.
